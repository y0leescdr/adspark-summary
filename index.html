Proximity & Value Analysis

A machine learning model that quantifies the impact of proximity to various amenities (schools, transit, parks) on property or commercial value.

Key Benefits for AdSpark:
  • Audience Lifestyle Insights: Understand what types of amenities are valued most in different neighborhoods to tailor messaging.
  • Contextual Ad Triggers: Trigger ads for home improvement, moving services, or local businesses when users are near high-value amenities.
  • Inform Client Messaging: Help real estate or retail clients highlight key location-based advantages in their marketing materials.

Automated Asset & POI Mapping

A solution using Gemini AI to automatically detect and map physical assets from millions of Street View images and triangulate their precise coordinates.

Key Benefits for AdSpark:
  • Map OOH Inventory: Automate finding and mapping OOH advertising assets (e.g., billboards, transit shelters) that may not be in standard databases.
  • Competitor Asset Monitoring: Systematically identify and geolocate competitor storefronts, signage, or promotional materials in a given area.
  • Contextual Targeting: Build custom maps of Points of Interest (POIs) relevant to a campaign (e.g., "all sari-sari stores in this district") for highly contextual ads.

Intelligent Geocoding Assistant

An AI agent that analyzes and corrects messy, real-world addresses, resolving them to precise geographic coordinates with high accuracy.

Key Benefits for AdSpark:
  • Maximize Campaign ROI: Ensure location-based ads are delivered to the correct physical areas by validating customer address data.
  • Reduce Wasted Ad Spend: Eliminate budget waste from targeting incorrect or unresolvable locations in geofenced campaigns.
  • Enhance Data Quality: Standardize and enrich your client's first-party location data, making it a more valuable asset for segmentation and analysis.

Hyper-Local Audience Clustering

A data pipeline that processes thousands of delivery points to identify statistically significant customer clusters, revealing true audience hotspots.

Key Benefits for AdSpark:

  • Identify "True" Trade Areas: Visualize where customers are most dense, providing insights for both digital campaigns and physical site planning for clients.
  • Optimize OOH & Retail Media: Inform Out-of-Home media buying by identifying high-value ad locations based on proven customer density.
